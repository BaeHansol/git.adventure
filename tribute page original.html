Sun-kyun Lee

Mar.1975-Dec.2023

In memory of his pure soul

Who is
Sun-kyun Lee?

A South Korean actor who has appeared in various TV series and movies. An actor who did his best in every role, whether it was a supporting role or a leading role. An actor who treated people in a polite manner in a harsh filming environment and was kind to small roles and staff.

  • 1975 Born in Seoul,South Korea
  • 2001 Debut as a supporting actor in a TV series
  • 2007 He was a forgotten actor, in 2007 received a lot of attention when he successfully played the role of Choi Do-yeong in “White Tower.”
  • He is evaluated as an actor who shows a stable style in a wide range of areas, from hard dramas to romantic comedies.
  • 2019 he appeared in director Bong Joon-ho's film "Parasite" and received favorable reviews for his performance that suited the role of President Park.
  • 2023 After being arrested by the police on charges of drug possession in December 2023, he committed suicide unable to overcome the pressure of the investigation.
